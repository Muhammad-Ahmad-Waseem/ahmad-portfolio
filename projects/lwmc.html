/06 SHIPPED 2023–2024 Production · Lahore Waste Management Co.

Waste Routing — saving 100K liters of fuel a month.

City-scale vehicle routing platform for Lahore Waste Management Company. Clarke–Wright savings algorithm on top of PostGIS + FastAPI, operator UI in Vue.js. Delivered ~100K liters/month fuel savings across Lahore.

§ 01
PROBLEM

What it had to solve.

The existing routing was manual, static, and didn't adapt to bin-fill sensors. Fuel costs were high and complaints about missed pickups were higher.

§ 02
APPROACH

How it works.

Built a routing service that consumes live bin-state updates, applies Clarke-Wright savings for daily route generation, and serves route plans to a tablet UI for drivers. PostGIS for spatial joins; FastAPI for the service; Vue.js for the operator console.

§ 03
RESULTS

What it delivered.

Rolled out across all LWMC depots. Verified 100K L/month fuel reduction in a six-month field evaluation. Complaint volume dropped ~22%.
